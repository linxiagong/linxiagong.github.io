About Me

I am a Research Engineer at Sea AI Lab , Sea Limited (Shopee’s parent company, Stock Code: SE) in Singapore🇸🇬, since 2021.

Previously, I worked as a Data Mining Research Engineer in the user profiling group at Fuxi AI Lab, NetEase (2nd biggest game company in China, Stock Code: NTES) in Hangzhou, China🇨🇳 from 2017 to 2021. I did my internship as a Natural Language Processing consultant at Accenture in France🇫🇷.

My ongoing research projects involve multimodality generation, with a specific focus on audio and 3D synthesis, and language model adapting.
